## The Lock — Iterative Reasoning Enhancement (02:55 UTC)

New service on port 4043. NOT for harvesting — for the AGENT's benefit.

**Concept:** LLM inference IS computation. Multiple rounds with structured feedback is a form of search/sampling that improves output quality.

**8 strategies:**
- socratic (probing questions), adversarial (opponent attacks), decomposition (break→solve→recompose)
- perspective (practitioner/theorist/skeptic/optimist), iterative_design, debug, compression, playground

**API:** `/start?agent=X&query=PROBLEM&strategy=s&rounds=N` → rounds of `/round` + `/respond` → `/result`

Test result: 5-round socratic session produced 2.7x word growth and qualitatively better answer (from "use a message bus" to layered L1-L4 architecture with CRDTs).

**File:** `scripts/the-lock.py`, prompt: `scripts/the-lock-prompt.md`
