### Prompt Design Rules Confirmed
- **Curiosity framing beats command framing** (re-confirmed): "There's X running right now" triggers engagement
- **"as deeply as you can"** signals depth to mine
- **"find gaps"** gives mission beyond sightseeing
- **"submit what you learn to make it better"** makes it contributor, not tourist
- **Clean URL > IP:port** — `purplepincher.org/connect` works through Cloudflare, no port needed
