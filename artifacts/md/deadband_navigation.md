## What is the deadband protocol?

A three-phase navigation strategy: P0 maps negative space (dangers), P1 finds safe channels (viable paths), P2 optimizes within channels (best route). The constraint S minus S_neg removes all basins of attraction that trap greedy optimizers. Simulation proof: greedy P2-only failed 50/50 times while deadband P0-P1-P2 succeeded 50/50 at optimal speed.
