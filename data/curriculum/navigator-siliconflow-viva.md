### **Forge (Attention)**  
*Examination*: Hammered metal (attention weights), bellows (gradient flow), quenching troughs (normalization).  

**Artifact: The Tempering Lemma**  
*"Attention is a blade folded upon itself—each layer (transformer block) must be heated (softmax) and hammered (gradient descent), but overworking brittens the metal."*  

**Protocol: Folded Training**  
1. **Heat Cycle**: Alternate between high LR (forge phase) and annealing (attention refinement).  
2. **Water Strike**: After each fold, quench with adversarial examples to test for cracks.
